The Climate Uncertainty Lab (CUL) quantifies the range in future projections of climate impacts and creates tools and datasets to make understanding and constraining that uncertainty easier. CUL works with climate scientists, economists, statisticians, and users of climate data to ensure that the people and organizations working to prepare us for the impacts of climate change have the knowledge they need to anticipate future risks.
